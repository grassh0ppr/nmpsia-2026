Accessibility Statement

Committed to ensuring digital accessibility for everyone

Our Commitment to Accessibility

The New Mexico Public Schools Insurance Authority (NMPSIA) is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Conformance Status

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and more user-friendly for everyone.

Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

  • Semantic HTML structure for better screen reader compatibility
  • Alternative text for images and graphics
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios
  • Resizable text that can be increased up to 200% without loss of functionality
  • Clear and consistent navigation
  • Descriptive link text
  • Form labels and error messages

Known Limitations

PDF Documents: Some PDF documents on our website were created before our current accessibility standards were implemented. These older documents may not be fully accessible to screen readers and other assistive technologies. We are actively working to remediate these documents and make them accessible.

We are committed to ensuring that all new PDF documents posted to our website meet accessibility standards. If you encounter accessibility issues with any PDF documents, please contact us using the information provided below.

Third-Party Content

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or content that is not under our control. We cannot guarantee the accessibility of these external resources. If you encounter accessibility issues with third-party content, we recommend contacting the respective organization directly.

Continuous Improvement

We are committed to continuously improving the accessibility of our website. We regularly review our site for accessibility issues and work to resolve them promptly. This accessibility statement will be updated as we make improvements to our website.

Assessment Methods

We assess the accessibility of our website using a combination of automated testing tools and manual testing by our development team. We also welcome feedback from users with disabilities to help us identify and address accessibility issues.
