STOPit! Solutions: STOPit! is an Anonymous Reporting System that is offered free of charge to all NMPSIA members and allows individuals in crisis to reach out for immediate help via a web App, website and text. Along with the Anonymous Reporting System, though, the STOPit! App is full of resources and information on a variety of topics, including Mental Health and Wellbeing. This information is customized to your local School/District. If your School/District is not already using STOPit!, we hope you will encourage your Administration to adopt this important tool and resource: https://www.stopitsolutions.com/
