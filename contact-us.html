Who You Gonna Call? Your Quick Contact Guide

Not sure who to contact? Use this guide to find the right department for your inquiry.

Important note: The EMPLOYER is always your first point of contact.

Employer

  • All general inquiries
  • Enrollment questions
  • Benefit questions
  • Eligibility verification
  • Premium payment issues
  • Forms and documentation

Always contact your employer first before reaching out to other departments.

Erisa

NMPSIA Eligibility Administrative Office

  • Enrollment issues
  • Eligibility questions
  • Premium billing
  • Premium collection
  • COBRA Continuation (medical, dental, or vision)
  • Retiree life continuation
  • Other administrative inquiries

Carrier

Insurance Providers

  • Claim submissions
  • Claim status inquiries
  • Provider network questions
  • Coverage benefits clarification
  • ID card requests
  • Pre-authorization requests
  • Plan-specific questions

NMPSIA

New Mexico Public Schools Insurance Authority

  • Benefit questions
  • Risk questions
  • Program information
  • Policy inquiries
  • General NMPSIA information
